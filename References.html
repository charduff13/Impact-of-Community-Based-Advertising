About the Project

This research paper was written collaboratively by four group members in COMM 320 - Media Ecosystem class in Fall 2018. As part of a semester long project-based learning, students explored one media outlet in Chicago, one community in Chicago and their reciprocal relations in the Chicago media ecosystem. The students conducted a social power analysis to examine the economical, ideological and political power relations between the media outlet and its audience in the community. Following guest lectures from experts in the city and with research at the media outlet, the students learned about the Chicago media ecosystem.

For more information, please contact Dr. Yonty Friesem, Assistant Professor and Director of Graduate studies at the Communication Department, School of Media Arts.
